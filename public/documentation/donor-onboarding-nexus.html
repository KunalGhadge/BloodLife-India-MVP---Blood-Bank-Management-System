08. Managing Our Hero Network (Donors)

Donors are the backbone of any blood bank. The Donor Management module is designed to make it as easy as possible to register new donors and maintain their records over time.

Core Value: Donor Care. We focus on keeping donor data organized so that they can be contacted efficiently and respectfully.

Registering a New Donor

When onboarding a new donor, we collect basic but vital information:

  • Personal Details: Name, Contact Info, and Location.
  • Medical Essentials: Blood Group and any relevant health markers.
  • Availability: Whether they are currently able to donate or on a "break" (e.g., due to travel or medication).

The Validation Pipeline

Before a donor is added to the "Active" list, the system performs several checks:

  1. Data Completion: Are all mandatory fields filled?
  2. Age Check: Most regions require donors to be between 18 and 65 years old.
  3. Duplicate Check: We ensure that the same donor isn't registered multiple times.

Donor Privacy

donor information is sensitive. BloodLife keeps this data primarily local to the device being used, ensuring that your hero network's contact details aren't floating around unsecured servers.

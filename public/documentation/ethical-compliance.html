24. Ethics and Responsibility

Managing records of blood donation is a sacred responsibility. We hold ourselves to the highest ethical standards when it comes to data privacy and the management of biological resources.

Our Pledge: Altruism Over Profit. BloodLife is and will always be a tool for the public good.

Data Stewardship

We believe that your data belongs to you. We do not sell or share donor information with third-party advertisers. Our local-first approach ensures that sensitive contact details are kept out of massive, centralized databases.

Fair Allocation

The system is designed to provide fair, unbiased donor matching based purely on medical compatibility and urgency. We explicitly forbid any algorithms that prioritize requests based on non-medical factors.

Respecting Donors

Donors are the heroes of this system. We ensure that their privacy is protected and that they are only contacted for genuine, medical needs. We provide clear ways for donors to "opt-out" or take a break from the active registry at any time.
